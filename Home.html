Achievements of the Barangay

Adolescent-Friendly Health Facility

In November 2024, Barangay Balatas was officially recognized as a Certified Adolescent-Friendly Health Facility, underscoring its commitment to providing accessible and inclusive healthcare services for young people.

Road Safety Education

The Sangguniang Kabataan of Balatas successfully conducted a Free Bike Seminar on Road Safety in June 2025 to educate youth and indigent students on bicycle maintenance, proper riding behavior, and road safety.

Development Hub

The barangay hosts the Balatas Development Complex, which includes the PAGCOR Multipurpose Evacuation Center and serves as a site for other important future development projects for Naga City.
