The new development in Balatas

The Balatas New Development Area is a key site for socialized housing, featuring the multi-rise condominium project "The Legacy Residences" under the national Pambansang Pabahay Para sa Pilipino (4PH) Housing Program.

The transformation of Barangay Balatas includes the successful closure and rehabilitation of the former city dumpsite, which is now being developed into the Urban Agriculture Training Center and Demo Farm.

This undertaking represents a major environmental turnaround and underscores the community's commitment to food security and green initiatives through modernized urban agriculture.

Complete with a new 20-meter wide road network that connects the business district to the uptown barangays of Naga, Balatas New Development Area will serve as a commercial expansion to the bustling Magsaysay Avenue.

With a new hospital, public schools, and government offices in place, Balatas will draw in commercial traffic in the location. The PNP and BFP headquarters, Local agri-processing zones, and state-of-the-art command centers are great additions to this area.

The Balatas Development Complex acts as a new civic services hub, decentralizing city operations by housing major public facilities. It includes the 200-bed Naga City General Hospital, the Center for Safety and Resiliency (CeSaR), the Police (NCPO) and Fire (BFP) offices, and the Balatas National High School.
