About us

We are students from the University of Nueva Caceres developing a Family Database Management System for Barangay Balatas, Naga City. Our goal is to improve local governance by streamlining record-keeping, enhancing transparency, and ensuring efficient service delivery to all residents.

Sustainable Development Goals

SDG 1: NO POVERTY

By ensuring efficient and equitable distribution of social aid through a digital tracking system.

SDG 11: SUSTAINABLE CITIES AND COMMUNITIES

By providing a tool for more effective and data-driven urban planning and governance at the community level.

SDG 16: PEACE, JUSTICE AND STRONG INSTITUTIONS

By promoting transparency, accountability, and the rule of law through better data management and public service delivery.

What is our mission & vision?

Mission

To ensure peace and order, promote awareness, and provide opportunities for small businesses and entrepreneurs through livelihood projects and community development, thereby creating a model to the barangay.

Vision

A progressive Barangay Balatas that is peaceful, clean, and fosters a strong sense of community and equality.
